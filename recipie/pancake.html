pancake

Pancakes are a beloved breakfast food enjoyed in various forms across cultures. Typically, They're flat, round, and cooked on a griddle or skillet. The batter is made from a mixture of flour, eggs, milk (or buttermilk), a leavening agent (like baking powder), and a hint of sweetness (sugar or sometimes flavored with vanilla). This batter is poured onto a hot cooking surface and cooked until golden brown on both sides.

Their texture can vary from fluffy and light to slightly denser, depending on the recipe and ingredients used. They're often served stacked on a plate, with each layer separated by a drizzle of syrup (such as maple syrup) or toppings like butter, fruit, chocolate chips, whipped cream, or nuts.
